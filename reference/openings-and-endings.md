# 8 种开头 + 8 种结尾（替代 Nowadays / In conclusion）

开头结尾是阅卷老师扫得最仔细的两处。一个让人眼前一亮的开头能让你的作文从 8 分档直接被往 11 分档扔。

---

## 开头：8 种高分套路

### 1. 现象速写（最推荐，万能场景）

> Walk into any classroom today, and one sight repeats itself: heads bowed, eyes locked onto glowing screens.

**适用**：科技、教育、生活方式题
**骨架**：Walk into any [X], and one sight [is impossible to miss / repeats itself]: [描写].

### 2. 数据反差

> A recent survey suggests that the average undergraduate now spends more time scrolling than sleeping — a figure that would have seemed dystopian a decade ago, yet now scarcely raises an eyebrow.

**适用**：任何能扯上"现代生活"的题
**骨架**：A recent [study/survey/report] suggests that [惊人数字] — a figure that [反差感].

### 3. 对比设问

> A book demands patience; a screen rewards distraction. Which, then, is truly shaping the way we think?

**适用**：二选一题、对比题
**骨架**：[X 短句]; [Y 短句反差]. Which, then, ...?

### 4. 引人入胜的小故事（轶事开头）

> Last Tuesday, I watched a stranger on the subway weep — quietly, eyes still fixed on his phone screen, finger still scrolling. I have not stopped thinking about him since.

**适用**：人际、情感、个人成长题
**注意**：要"我观察到的小细节"，不要"我有一个朋友 …"

### 5. 反驳通常假设

> We are told, repeatedly, that reading is in decline. The data, awkwardly, suggest otherwise.

**适用**：观点对立题、需要反主流的题
**骨架**：We are told that [common claim]. [转折], the [evidence] suggests otherwise.

### 6. 拆解题目关键词

> The phrase 'lifelong learning' has been so widely repeated that it now risks meaning nothing at all. Yet beneath the cliché lies a question this generation cannot avoid: what does it actually mean to keep learning, when the very skills we acquire grow obsolete within a decade?

**适用**：抽象概念题、谚语题
**骨架**：The phrase '[X]' has been so widely [repeated/invoked] that ... Yet beneath the cliché lies ...

### 7. 历史回响

> Each technological wave — print, radio, television — promised to liberate the mind, and each, in turn, was accused of dulling it. Artificial intelligence is merely the latest chapter in an old story.

**适用**：科技、文化变迁题
**骨架**：Each [wave/generation] — [三个例子] — promised X, and each, in turn, [反讽].

### 8. 引言 + 反驳（要小心用，不要烂大街引言）

> Susan Sontag once observed that 'a library is a tool for shaping the future.' She might be dismayed to see what we are shaping it into.

**适用**：文化、阅读、教育题
**注意**：不要用 "As Confucius said" 或滥俗名言

---

## 结尾：8 种高分套路

### 1. 隐喻总结（最易出彩）

> If knowledge is the compass and curiosity the wind, then deliberate reading remains the boat we choose to board — without it, we do not so much navigate the modern world as drift through it.

**骨架**：If X is the [Y], and Z is the [W], then [主体] remains the [载体] — without it, we ...

### 2. 回扣开头

> Returning to the café we entered at the outset: the heads remain bowed. The question is no longer whether technology will dominate our attention, but whether we will reclaim it.

**前提**：开头用了"现象速写"，结尾才能回扣
**威力**：阅卷老师看到 "Returning to ..." 会忍不住翻回开头看，是一种刻意的"召回"

### 3. 让步 + 立场（最适合议论文）

> To be sure, no single individual can reverse the trend overnight. But neither can such reversal occur without us — each small refusal, each quieter footprint, accumulates into the change we seek.

**骨架**：To be sure, [让步]. But neither can [反转] without [主体] — [具体行动].

### 4. 改写谚语 / 给出修正版

> The proverb deserves an amendment: failure is the mother of success — but reflection is the midwife.

**适用**：谚语题专用，超出彩

### 5. 警告式收尾

> A society that outsources its thinking to algorithms may eventually find that it has forgotten how to think at all. The choice, while still ours, will not remain so forever.

**适用**：警示性话题（科技、环境、依赖）

### 6. 升华式提问

> The question is not whether technology will continue to advance — that much is certain. The question is whether we will keep up not in productivity, but in wisdom.

**骨架**：The question is not whether [X — that much is certain]. The question is whether [Y].

### 7. 时间感收尾

> Twenty years from now, our generation will not be judged by how cleverly we mastered our tools, but by how carefully we mastered ourselves.

**骨架**：Twenty/Fifty years from now, [主体] will be judged not by X, but by Y.

### 8. 简洁有力（适合字数告急）

> What remains, then, is not a question of capability but of will.

**适用**：写到结尾发现没词了，留一个利落短句，胜过硬凑

---

## 配对建议（开头 + 结尾呼应）

| 开头 | 最佳结尾搭配 |
|------|-------------|
| 现象速写 | 回扣开头（最强配对！） |
| 数据反差 | 警告式 / 升华式提问 |
| 对比设问 | 让步 + 立场 |
| 轶事故事 | 时间感收尾 |
| 反驳假设 | 让步 + 立场 |
| 拆解关键词 | 改写概念 / 隐喻总结 |
| 历史回响 | 时间感 / 警告式 |
| 引言 + 反驳 | 升华式提问 |

---

## 最后一条提醒

**写完作文后**：去掉开头第一句和结尾最后一句，看看作文还成立吗？如果还成立，说明这两句没起作用，要么改强它，要么换掉。
**好的开头和结尾应该是"少了它整篇都垮"的存在。**
