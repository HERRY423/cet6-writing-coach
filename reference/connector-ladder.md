# 连接词梯度表

按"低（8 分）→ 中（11 分）→ 高（14 分）"分档，按功能分类。
**用法铁律**：一篇 150 词的作文里，**同一功能不要重复用同一个连接词**。比如表"而且"已经用 moreover，下一段就用 what is more 或 equally important。

---

## 一、表"增补 / 而且"

| 8 分 | 11 分 | 14 分 |
|------|-------|-------|
| also, and, besides | moreover, furthermore, in addition | what is more, equally important, on top of that, not to mention |

升级提示：
- **What is more, ...** 比 moreover 更口语化、更地道
- **Equally telling is the fact that ...** 用于引出一个"同样有力的证据"
- **Add to this ..., and the picture becomes ...** 用于第二段过渡到第三段

---

## 二、表"转折 / 然而"

| 8 分 | 11 分 | 14 分 |
|------|-------|-------|
| but, however | nevertheless, nonetheless | that said, be that as it may, yet for all its merits, then again |

升级提示：
- **That said, ...** 是 native 最高频的让步衔接，强烈推荐
- **Yet for all its X, ...** 用于引出反驳论点
- **Be that as it may, ...** 略正式，议论文优选

---

## 三、表"因果"

| 8 分 | 11 分 | 14 分 |
|------|-------|-------|
| because, so, since | therefore, thus, consequently, as a result | given that, in light of, owing to, by virtue of, this is why ... |

升级提示：
- **Given that X, Y becomes inevitable.** 用于推论
- **In light of recent X, ...** 用于结合背景
- **By virtue of X, Y ...** 强调"凭借"

---

## 四、表"举例"

| 8 分 | 11 分 | 14 分 |
|------|-------|-------|
| for example, such as | for instance, namely, in particular | a case in point is, consider for instance, one needs look no further than X |

升级提示：
- **A case in point is ...** 比 for example 高级一档
- **One needs look no further than X to see this ...** 用于强例
- **Consider, for a moment, ...** 引导读者思考

---

## 五、表"结论"

| 8 分 | 11 分 | 14 分 |
|------|-------|-------|
| in conclusion, to sum up, in a word | overall, in essence | ultimately, when all is said and done, what this comes down to, to bring this argument full circle |

升级提示：
- **Ultimately, ...** 简洁有力，绝对优于 in conclusion
- **What this all amounts to is ...** 适合总结复杂论证
- **To bring this argument full circle ...** 配合"回扣开头"结尾用

---

## 六、表"对比"

| 8 分 | 11 分 | 14 分 |
|------|-------|-------|
| compared with, on the other hand | in contrast, by comparison, conversely | whereas X confines itself to Y, Z ventures into ... / X and Y stand at opposite poles |

## 七、表"递进 / 程度更高"

| 8 分 | 11 分 | 14 分 |
|------|-------|-------|
| more importantly | even more crucially | above all, more troubling still, crucially, what makes this more striking is ... |

## 八、表"个人观点引入"

| 8 分 | 11 分 | 14 分 |
|------|-------|-------|
| I think, in my opinion | from my perspective, to my mind, personally speaking | I would venture to argue that, it is my firm conviction that, to my mind, the most compelling case is ... |

---

## 九、表"让步（11 → 14 的关键）"

这一栏**必须每篇作文用一个**，是冲分核心：

- Granted, X ... but Y ...
- Critics may counter that X. They are not wrong about Y; they are wrong about Z.
- Admittedly, X. Yet to dismiss Y on these grounds is to miss the larger point.
- To be sure, X has its limits. But ...
- While it is true that X, this hardly invalidates Y.

---

## 十、避雷

- ❌ 通篇 firstly / secondly / finally 出现三次以上
- ❌ 同一句里塞两个连接词（however ... but ...）
- ❌ Last but not least（已死）
- ❌ All in all（已死）
- ❌ to make a long story short（不正式）

---

**实操方案**：写完作文做一次"连接词扫描"：
1. 找出所有连接词
2. 看有没有重复
3. 至少有一个"让步连接词"
4. 总数控制在 5-7 个（不要每句都用）
