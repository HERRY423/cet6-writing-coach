# 示例 01：粘段作文求批改

> 真实场景演示。学生粘了一段 CET-6 议论文（约 150 词），看教练给什么。
> 题目假设：The importance of mental health for college students.

---

## 学生原文

```
Nowadays, with the rapid development of society, more and more people pay
attention to mental health, especially college students. It is very important
for them. There are many reasons. First, if students have mental problems,
they cannot study well. Second, mental health can make their life happy.
Third, every coin has two sides, so we should pay attention to it. As we all
know, mental health is important. In a word, in my opinion, college students
should pay attention to their mental health. Last but not least, the
government and schools should help them.
```

---

## 教练的回复

### 印象分

**8/15**（六级作文折算 ≈ 57 分，整卷折算 ≈ 92–96）

档位特征对得上：主从复合句少、用词大量重复（important × 3, pay attention to × 3,
people × 2）、论证空洞（"如果……就不能" + "可以让生活快乐"）、模板痕迹明显。

### 四色标注

| 色 | 原句 | 问题 |
|---|---|---|
| 🟡 | Nowadays, with the rapid development of society | 阅卷重点避雷开头，**直接扣印象分** |
| 🟡 | more and more people | → a growing number of |
| 🟡 | very important | → pivotal / paramount / indispensable |
| 🟡 | There are many reasons | → Several factors account for / This stems from |
| 🔴 | Mental health can make their life happy | `make + 名词 + 形容词`搭配勉强，且逻辑跳跃（心理健康→生活快乐？还需要桥梁论证） |
| 🟡 | Every coin has two sides | **烂大街禁用**，且与本段论点无关，纯凑字 |
| 🟡 | As we all know | 阅卷已避雷 |
| 🟡 | In a word, in my opinion | 两个冗余开场叠用 |
| 🟡 | Last but not least | 阅卷已避雷 |
| 🟢 | "the government and schools should help them" | 结尾想升华到社会层面，方向对，但表达太轻 |
| 🔵 | "if students have mental problems, they cannot study well" | 想用因果，思路是对的，只是说法太粗 |

### 小幅修改版（只动 🔴🟡，保留你 80% 的内容）

> Mental health has emerged as a pressing concern among college students. **A
> growing number of** students report symptoms of anxiety and depression, and
> **this trend stems from several factors**. First, untreated mental distress
> directly undermines academic performance. Second, sustained psychological
> well-being is **pivotal** to a fulfilling campus life. **From my perspective,**
> college students should prioritise their mental health, and **both
> institutions and government bodies bear a shared responsibility** in
> supporting them.

— 保留你的三段式骨架、三个 reason、政府/学校的结尾思路；只换烂大街表达 + 删冗余开场。

### 冲分改写版（加 🟢 可升级点：物称主语 / 长句打包 / 短句爆发 / 让步反驳）

> **Mental health, once relegated to the periphery of campus discourse, now
> sits at the heart of student well-being.**¹ Untreated distress quietly
> erodes both academic engagement and the capacity to seek help — a vicious
> spiral that **few transcripts ever record**.² Critics may counter that
> resilience is forged precisely by adversity; **yet adversity without support
> is not training but injury.**³ The remedy is neither lone willpower nor
> sweeping policy alone: campus counselling, peer networks and family
> conversations must **converge into a single safety net.**⁴ **The cost of
> silence is too steep to bear.**⁵

**💡 给分点注释：**
1. **物称主语 + 历史化对照**（Mental health, once... now sits at the heart of...）→ 替代 "Nowadays" 开头
2. **物称主语 + 抽象名词化**（Untreated distress... erodes... — a vicious spiral that few transcripts ever record）→ 取代"如果 A，就 B"
3. **让步反驳 / 钢人论证**（Critics may counter that... yet...）→ 这是 13 分以上的关键得分点，本来原文完全没有
4. **长句信息打包**（campus counselling, peer networks and family conversations must converge into...）→ 替代"政府和学校应该帮"
5. **短句爆发收尾**（The cost of silence is too steep to bear.）→ 替代 In conclusion

### 最该练的 1 个弱点

> **用万能词当主语**（people, students, we, everyone）。
>
> 这是把你卡在 8 分的最大原因——开头三个段全是"more and more people / students / we"。
> 训练方法：从这周开始，写任何主旨句都强迫自己**用事物或现象当主语**（Mental health /
> The phenomenon / This trend / The decision），动词自动会升维，分数自然上来。
